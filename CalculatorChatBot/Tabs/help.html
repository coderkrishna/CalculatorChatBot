Calculator Chat Bot

The content below will show a quick help guide to what this chat bot can actually do

Arithmetic

Addition

This is the simple addition of the numbers in the list that you provide

Subtraction

This is the simple difference among the numbers in the list that you provide

Multiplication

This is the simple product of the numbers in the list that you provide. Note that if you actually enter 0 as one of the numbers, the product overall becomes 0

Division

This is the quotient of two numbers in the list that you provide

Remainder

This is the remainder of two numbers in the list that you provide


Statistics

Mean

Given the list of numbers that you provide, the bot will then calculate the average. First the sum of all numbers gets calculated. Then the bot will divide that sum by the size of the list

Median

The bot will find the middle of the list in the following manner:

  • The list of numbers could either be in order, or be out of order. So, the bot would then make sure that the list that you provide is sorted in order
  • Once the numbers have been sorted in order, the bot will then find the middle element of the list. If the list is odd length, then the bot can find the median in a straight forward manner. However, if the list is an even length, then the median is calculated as the average of the two middle elements of the list.
Mode

This is the simple product of the numbers in the list that you provide. Note that if you actually enter 0 as one of the numbers, the product overall becomes 0

Range
